For XLK (a liquid, large ETF), good intraday signals are:
    -Price trend (short-term momentum)
    -Volume confirmation
    -Moving averages (trend bias)
    -VWAP (intraday fair value)
    -Volatility expansion (breakouts)

Bot should monitor every 1–5 minutes:

-Trend Bias:
    9 EMA
    20 EMA
    50 EMA
-Rules:
    9 > 20 > 50 → bullish bias
    9 < 20 < 50 → bearish bias

-Volume Strength
    Current volume vs 20-period average volume
    Volume spike = breakout confirmation

-Candlestick Features (Quantifiable)
    Instead of “looks bullish,” encode:
    Body size
    Wick size ratio
    Bullish engulfing (close > previous high)
    Bearish engulfing (close < previous low)

-VWAP (Very Important for Intraday)
    Price above VWAP → institutions likely long bias
    Price below VWAP → sell pressure bias

-Momentum
    RSI (14)
    MACD histogram slope


-Process Structure
Data Collector (Alpaca API)
        ↓
Feature Generator
        ↓
Signal Scoring Engine
        ↓
Decision Logic
        ↓
Risk Manager
        ↓
Execution

-Scoring System
| Signal            | Condition | Score |
| ----------------- | --------- | ----- |
| EMA Alignment     | Bullish   | +2    |
| Volume Spike      | Yes       | +1    |
| Above VWAP        | Yes       | +1    |
| RSI between 50–70 | Yes       | +1    |
| Bearish Engulfing | Yes       | -2    |

-Risk Management
    Max 1–2 trades per hour
    Risk only 0.5–1% per trade
    Hard stop loss (never optional)
    Stop trading after 2 consecutive losses
    No overnight holds (for now)
